# Storage

This discovery domain explains how storage is built, presented, and operating inside the Azure Local environment.

## Scope

It should capture:

- S2D status and health
- pool composition and media distribution
- cache and capacity relationships
- virtual disks and resiliency details
- CSV layout and ownership
- SOFS where applicable
- faults, repair jobs, and storage health indicators
- QoS and replication features when configured

## Why It Matters

Storage design and health are central to both operational understanding and as-built documentation.
