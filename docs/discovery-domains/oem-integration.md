# OEM Integration

This discovery domain explains vendor-specific tooling and support integrations tied to the Azure Local hardware platform.

## Scope

It should capture relevant management and lifecycle tooling for vendors such as Dell, Lenovo, HPE, and DataON, along with informative handling of whitebox environments.

## Why It Matters

OEM tooling often carries important lifecycle, firmware, compliance, and support signals that should be part of the complete environment story.
